Video

I found this video to be very enlightening as it not only focuses on the building itself - describing the
uniqueness of it compared to other buildings in Beijing's central business district, but the video also follows
Koolhaas and his team through the bidding process, in which CCTV had to decide which building design and architecture
firm to go with out of dozens of applicants.

It is interesting to know the context surrounding this decision. This decision was made by CCTV in 2001. By then,
CCTV was becoming far too large for its current building, built in the 80s. Beijing had also recently been awarded the 2008
Olympic games in 2001, which fueled a need to "upgrade" the city, showcasing the strong economic progress the country
was making at the time.
